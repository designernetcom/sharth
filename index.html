Laser Cutting

Laser cutting is a highly accurate manufacturing technique used to cut, engrave, or etch materials using a focused laser beam.

CNC Bending

CNC bending is a precise process used to bend sheet metal into specific shapes, angles, or complex geometries.

OXYFUEL Cutting

Oxyfuel cutting (also known as oxy-acetylene or flame cutting) is a metal-cutting process that uses oxygen and a fuel gas, usually acetylene, to produce a high-temperature flame that melts and cuts through metal.

Read More

TURRET Punching

Turret punching is a versatile metal fabrication process that uses a CNC (Computer Numerical Control) turret punch press to punch shapes or holes in sheet metal.

Read More

SHOT Blasting

Shot blasting is a mechanical cleaning process that propels abrasive materials at high speeds onto a surface to remove contaminants, rust, paint, or scale, and to prepare the surface for subsequent treatments like painting, coating, or welding.

Liquid Paint Booth

7 Tank PT With Dry Off Oven

The 7-Tank Phosphating Treatment (PT) with a Dry-Off Oven is a multi-stage metal surface preparation process used to clean, treat, and prime metal surfaces for painting or coating.

Powder Coating

Powder coating is a dry finishing process that applies a durable, attractive, and protective layer of coating to metal and other materials.
